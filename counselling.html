Welcome to Me In Time

Counselling in Manchester or Online

Sometimes we just need space — to breathe, to speak freely, and to feel truly heard. Counselling offers that space. Whether you’re navigating a specific challenge or holding something harder to name, this is time set aside for you: to explore, reflect, and begin to make sense of things at your own pace.

A Calm, Grounded Approach

I work integratively, drawing from a range of therapeutic approaches, but my work is always rooted in a person-centred foundation. That means we begin where you are — with no assumptions, no pressure, and no rush. I won’t diagnose or analyse you from a distance. Instead, we’ll build understanding together, in a relationship based on trust, safety, and respect.

Some of the Things We Might Explore

Sometimes we all need space to talk things through — to sit with the big, difficult questions and not have to face them alone.

  • Feeling anxious, low, or overwhelmed
  • Grief and loss
  • Low self-esteem or a harsh inner critic
  • Relationship difficulties
  • Questions around identity, purpose, or direction
  • Men’s mental health and the things that often go unsaid

Practicalities

Sessions last 50 minutes and are usually held weekly (though fortnightly can be arranged if that suits you better). I offer both in-person and online appointments.

Fee: £60 per session. A limited number of lower-cost spaces are available — please ask if this would help.

If you’d like to arrange an initial session or simply talk things through, please get in touch.
