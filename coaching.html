Coaching with Me In Time

Coaching is about getting unstuck — gently, honestly, and at your own pace. It offers a reflective space to name what matters, explore what’s in the way, and move forward in a way that feels grounded and real. No hype. No hustle. Just support that meets you where you are.

Not Just Goals — Direction

This isn’t about setting 10-year plans or “fixing” what’s wrong. It’s about paying attention to what’s already there — the small nudges, the quiet frustrations, the parts of you that are ready to move. Coaching can help you listen more closely, act more intentionally, and live in a way that feels more like you.

What We Might Work On

People come to coaching for all sorts of reasons:

  • Making a decision or navigating change
  • Feeling stuck, restless, or unsure what's next
  • Exploring purpose, creativity, or identity
  • Wanting to show up differently in work, life, or relationships
  • Reconnecting with confidence, voice, or ideas

Some come for a one-off Clarity Session. Others stay longer. We’ll work together in the way that suits you best.

What Does It Involve?

Coaching sessions last 60 minutes and are usually spaced fortnightly or monthly, depending on what feels helpful for you. We can meet online or in-person in Manchester — whichever suits your rhythm and routine.

Standard Fee: £70 per session. I also offer a longer, one-off 90-minute “Clarity Session” for £90 — a deeper dive if you're facing a specific decision or feeling stuck.

If you're curious or simply want to talk things through, you’re welcome to get in touch. There's no pressure — just a chance to explore whether this feels like the right fit for you.
