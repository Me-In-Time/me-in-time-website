Welcome to Me In Time

Thank you for dropping by. I know how hard it can be to reach out for support, and just being here is a meaningful first step. Whether you’re feeling overwhelmed, numb, stuck, or unsure where to begin — you don’t have to figure it out alone.

This space was created with care, for those moments when you need room to breathe, reflect, and be met as you are. I’m Stuart Walker, a counsellor and coach based in Manchester. I offer grounded, compassionate support that moves at your pace.

Read More

At the heart of Me In Time is a belief that change doesn’t need to be rushed. It begins by being seen, heard, and understood — without judgement or pressure. If that sounds like what you need right now, you’re in the right place.

Feel free to explore the site, read a little more, or drop me a message. There’s no pressure. Just a gentle space to begin — in your time.
